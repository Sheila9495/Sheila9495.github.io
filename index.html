About

Welcome to Fun For Kids! My name is Sheila Mirzad and I am a mom of two lovely daughters. I have always been in search of affordable, educational, and fun places to take my kids - places where they can learn, grow, and where we can spend quality time together. I used to spend hours every day searching for these activities and resources, and I always wished there was an easier way for parents to find them. That’s why I created this website: to help families discover safe, fun, and enriching local activities without the stress of endless searching. This website will help you easily find safe, affordable, and exciting activities in the local area. Here’s my daughter Diana's picture where she is enjoying Babytime at the Round Rock Public Library - one of our favorite spots to learn, play, and have fun together!
